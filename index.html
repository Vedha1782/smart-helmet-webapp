SUPPORT

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

HOW DO I LOGIN AS A RIDER?

Enter your Rider ID, scan your helmet QR code, and verify with the OTP sent to your registered mobile number.

WHAT IF MY HELMET IS NOT DETECTED?

Ensure the helmet is properly worn and the sensors are active. Contact support if the issue persists.

HOW DOES ALCOHOL DETECTION WORK?

The helmet contains breath sensors that detect alcohol levels. Any detection will prevent ride authorization.

WHO CAN ACCESS THE ADMIN DASHBOARD?

Only authorized company administrators with valid credentials can access the admin dashboard.
